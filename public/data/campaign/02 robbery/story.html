The surviving peasants after the battle tell you that the only former warrior among them died from the fangs of the beasts. They themselves are not accustomed to the sword, but you wielded it quite well, which is why you receive the weapon as a trophy. You are chosen as the leader of the group. It turns out that the peasants are languishing in the forest against their will. The Baron drove them from their land to breed sheep in their place. The neighboring lands are all occupied, with nowhere to go. Starvation awaits you.

Whether you like it or not, you have to resort to plundering.
